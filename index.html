The Initiative

What Is America Breathes 2028?

Founded by USMC Veteran and 20-year Postmaster David Blaesing, this is not a political campaign. It is a civic mission — listening first, proposing solutions second, and delivering results to Congress.

🎙️ Listen First

Public listening sessions at every tour stop with veterans, postal workers, rural residents, and everyday Americans sharing their real experiences.

📋 Document Everything

Every story, every challenge, every idea — documented through research, short films, and policy memos developed alongside student media teams.

🏛️ Deliver to Congress

Findings, reform proposals, and community stories delivered directly to policymakers in Washington, DC by November 2028 — no lobbyists, no political agenda.

🪖 Veterans First

The Stable Address Pilot Initiative centers veterans experiencing homelessness, ensuring they can receive critical mail, benefits, and services without a permanent address.

Flagship Program

The Stable Address Pilot Initiative

Every American deserves access to their mail — including those without a permanent address. This pilot program focuses on veterans, unsheltered Americans, and displaced families.

What the Pilot Does

  • Documents real barriers veterans face accessing mail and benefits
  • Maps how General Delivery, shelters, libraries, and Post Offices help or fail
  • Creates plain-language guides for receiving critical mail without a permanent address
  • Develops community referral pathways connecting veterans to VA, legal aid, and ID services
  • Produces policy memos for Congress and local leaders
  • Builds a scalable model for replication in all 50 states

Who This Serves

  • Homeless and unsheltered veterans
  • Displaced families and seniors
  • Americans rebuilding stability after hardship
  • Rural communities with limited postal access
  • Anyone who needs critical mail — benefits, medication, legal documents — without a fixed address
